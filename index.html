KOMOREBI BOY

An artist from Poland. He co-founded local hard rock bands "We don't know" and "PixieCut Lovers" created in 2017 and 2019. He was inspired by the music of lofi from the beginning of playing in rock bands - for several years he has been working independently, first offering lofi to other artists and companies, today recording his own songs.
